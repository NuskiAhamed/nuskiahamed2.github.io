Welcome to the branches of La Mirage Hotel, where our commitment to excellence and hospitality knows has no bounds. Branch Details

Adventure Park Safari Resort Water Park
Kandy Yala Avissawella
Our Adventure Park is a eye catching haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering plenty of exhilarating activities like zip lining, water sports and rock climbing. Set amidst stunning natural landscapes, it's the perfect place for adventure seekers of all ages to challenge themselves and make unforgettable memories. Join us for an adrenaline-filled day of excitement and exploration in the great outdoors! Our Safari Park is a captivating wildlife sanctuary, where you can embark on an unforgettable journey through open habitats filled with variety of animals from around the world. Witness various creatures up close, from majestic lions and graceful giraffes to playful elephants, all thriving in an environment that closely resembles their natural habitats. Immerse yourself in the wonder of the animal kingdom and create thrilling memories of wildlife encounters that will last a lifetime. Dive into a world of aquatic fun at our Water Park, where thrilling slides, lazy rivers, and wave pools promise a day of refreshing excitement for all ages. Whether you're seeking an adventure on our twisty water slides, a relaxing float along the lazy river, or a safe and splashing area for the little ones, our Water Park offers a range of attractions and experiences. Cool off, make a splash, and enjoy a day of wet and adventures with family and friends at our fantastic Water Park!
